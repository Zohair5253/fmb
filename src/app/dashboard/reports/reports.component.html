This is an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) for the FMB management application. Using which one can easily track the activities regarding FMB on day to day basis.

FMB use case is chosen with an intent to have a real-world use case to display features which could also be helpful in other scenarios as well.

For example: Maintaining Employees salary / leaves and other data, Maintaining stock of various items in your shop etc.

Similarly, there can be N number of use cases in which such applications will work as a catalyst to ease your day to day tasks.
